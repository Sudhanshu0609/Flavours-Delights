Classic Lasagna

Ingredients:

  1. 12 lasagna noodles,
  2. 1 pound ground beef
  3. 2 cups ricotta cheese
  4. 2 cups shredderd mozzarella cheese
  5. 2 cups marinara sauce
  6. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  7. salt and pepper to taste


Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 °F (190 °C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and ground beef. Cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and paper
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meatsauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, then a layer of ricotta cheese, and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
