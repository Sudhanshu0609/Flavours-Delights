Butter Chicken

Indulge in our rich and creamy Butter Chicken, a classic Indian delight that will leave you craving for more. Tender and juicy chicken cooked in a velvety tomato-based sauce, infused with the subtle flavors of cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. Finished with a generous dollop of butter and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, this dish is a masterclass in comfort food. Served with basmati rice or naan bread, our Butter Chicken is a must-try for anyone seeking a truly regal dining experience.
