Shahee Paneer

Shahee Paneer Indulge in the rich and creamy Shahee Paneer, a royal delight fit for kings and queens! Tender paneer (Indian cheese) is simmered in a luxurious sauce made with a blend of nuts, dried fruits, and warm spices, finished with a hint of cardamom and a drizzle of saffron. Served with basmati rice and naan bread, this dish is a masterclass in comfort food and opulence.
